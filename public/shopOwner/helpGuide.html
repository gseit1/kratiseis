Help Guide

Welcome to the help guide section. Here you will find useful information and tips on how to use the dashboard and manage your shop effectively.

Table Options

In the Table Options section, you can view and manage the tables in your shop. You can add new tables, edit existing ones, and delete tables that are no longer in use.

General Shop Details

The General Shop Details section allows you to view and edit the basic information about your shop, such as the shop name, description, phone number, and address.

Booking Hours

In the Booking Hours section, you can set and manage the hours during which your shop is open for bookings. Make sure to set your booking hours accurately to avoid any confusion with your customers.

Reservations

The Reservations section shows you all the reservations made for your shop. You can view the details of each reservation and manage their status (e.g., pending, accepted, completed, not shown).

Availability

In the Availability section, you can manage the availability of your tables. You can specify the days and hours when each table is available for booking.

Charts

The Charts section provides you with visual insights into your reservations data. You can view charts and graphs that show your reservations trends over time.

Photo Management

In the Photo Management section, you can upload, view, and delete photos related to your shop. Use this feature to keep your shop's photos up to date.

Support

If you encounter any issues or have any questions, you can reach out for support. We are here to help you!
